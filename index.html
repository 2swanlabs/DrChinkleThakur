FAQ

Frequently
Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?
Appointments are preferred so we can give you the time and attention you deserve. Walk-ins are also welcome based on availability. WhatsApp or call us to book quickly.
Is the treatment painful?
Dr. Chinkle uses gentle, modern techniques to minimise discomfort. Procedures like Root Canal Treatment are performed with care and local anaesthesia to keep you comfortable throughout.
Do you treat children?
Absolutely. We provide paediatric dental care in a calm, child-friendly environment that helps build positive habits and reduces dental anxiety from a young age.
How do I book an appointment?
The fastest way is to WhatsApp us at +91 82192 17200 with your name and preferred time. You can also call us directly. We confirm slots quickly.
What are your clinic hours?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:30 AM to 5:30 PM. We are closed on Sundays. Please call or WhatsApp beforehand to confirm your slot.
